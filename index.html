The Hidden Cost of
High-Achievement

There is a particular kind of exhaustion that the driven, the conscious, and the capable know intimately. It doesn't look like laziness from the outside. It looks like success. But inside, there is a tightness — a low-grade contraction — that never fully releases.

You may recognize it as the relentless mental chatter that follows you into stillness. The restlessness beneath the meditation cushion. The perfectionism that turns vision into pressure. The quiet burnout that shows up not as collapse, but as a subtle dimming of vitality, joy, and genuine aliveness.

What if the answer isn't more doing — but a radical deepening of your capacity to release?

II · The Method

Two Streams.
One Living Practice.

The Dissolve Mentorship weaves together two extraordinarily potent lineages into a single integrated arc of transformation.

💧

The Dissolve Method

A precise, awareness-based release practice passed down from Andy Mack — a 20-year veteran of Temple stays and deep committed study in the internal arts. Beginning with the feet, legs, and waist — the root that lightens the mind — we systematically release chronic physical, mental, and energetic contractions.

Dissolve is a relationship release process where we play with awareness and its object of focus. When we release the awareness, we release the object. The body, its energies, the thoughts — we see the relationships, and release.

⚔️

Heroic Clarity

Drawn from the Heroic.us framework, we illuminate your Wildly Important Goals, forge anti-fragile confidence, and channel your newfound spaciousness into purposeful, courageous action. We look at your Hero Bars, your heroes, your ultimate game — and we activate your Heroic self.

Whenever over-eagerness, self-doubt, or perfectionism arises during goal work, we immediately apply Dissolve — so growth remains spacious and embodied rather than tight or forced.

The Feedback Loop
Heroic Clarity → Direction & Courage → Dissolve → Release Contractions → Spacious Energy & Grounded Confidence → Better Action & Real Results

III · The Journey

The Arc of
Transformation

Over 2–6 months, each session has an integration period in between. Putting the methods into practice, taking adequate rest — we'll see real, measurable change.

1
Bodywork · 3 Sessions

Dissolve Therapy Sessions

Hands-on release work that gives you direct, lived knowledge of the Dissolve method. We work the torso in Session 1, the arms, neck, head & legs in Session 2, and the back in Session 3. Each session is 45–60 minutes. In-person (Barrie area) or deeply guided online.

2
Personal Training · 8 Sessions

Dissolve Training Sessions

Progressive 1:1 sessions teaching the Dissolve practice in standing, moving, and sitting postures. You learn to apply the qualities of Dissolve to body-opening exercises — and to life itself. Available online or in-person.

3
Workshop · 6 Hours or 4 Private Sessions

Heroic Clarity & Anti-Fragile Confidence

A deep dive into the frameworks that empower the self so it becomes Heroic. We explore Inspiring Goals, how to build anti-fragile confidence, and how to activate your ultimate self. Online or in-person.

4
Follow-Up · 4 Sessions

Heroic Clarity Personal Sessions

Following the workshop, I commit to 4 personal sessions to facilitate further progress on what emerged — applying it to real obstacles, real goals, and real life as it unfolds.

5
Community · Weekly (Optional)

Melting Mornings

Every Monday morning I host a group Dissolve session — "Solo Dissolve" — a whole-body release practice unlike yoga, meditation, or qigong. A powerful way to begin your week from spaciousness. Open to all cohort members.
