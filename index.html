The Practice

A practice of spacious awareness.

Dissolve is a consciousness-based practice that empowers you to see your own spacious nature. Through an exploration of awareness and its relationship to body and mind, you gain the tools to free yourself from mental, physical, and emotional constraints — and lighten your load.

Dissolve works with what is already here — your breath, your attention, your body, your present circumstances. The practice is seeing a quality of spaciousness that was always present beneath the contraction.

See
Soften the eyes. Notice what is here.

Let Go
Release without force. Allow what dissolves to dissolve.

Rest in the spaciousness. Feel the breath like honey.

About

Jordan

When I was younger I wanted to learn something magical. I saw taiji as that thing — subtle, yet powerful. The contrast of an old lady practicing tai chi in a chaotic YMCA environment inspired me to search for that quality: soft, not yielding, not imposing. A relaxed composure. I wanted that.

After practicing Tai Chi for a number of years I came across Andy Mack and Luang Por Jumnian. They were practicing something called Dissolve — a method of clear seeing and release. I became a student, trained one-on-one, and got certified as a Dissolve Therapist.

"In any battle, begin by fighting yourself, and you're already outnumbered."

Dissolve helped me stop fighting myself and resolve that conflict. Now I see the different facets of who I am and I release in relationship to them. Things are more integrated. I feel more whole. Far more peaceful than before. This is what I offer to you — not a concept, but a living breathing practice.
